A Few Words About Our Company

Who We Are

EUROHERBAL is a European company established for more than 30 years in the health service, determined by the natural methods, primarily to help people with health problems.


Mission

The mission of our company is not only to respond to the needs of our customers, but to constantly strive towards higher and better quality. We want to be the most trusted company in the scientific herbal medicine fields, and most admired for our ethics, values and commitment to sustainability.

At the heart of the Euroherbal Tea mission is the belief that good health should be accessible to everyone, and we strive to make this possible through our commitment to science-driven herbal healthcare.

Vision

Euroherbal vision is to successfully promote humanism in the quality of products and thereby to enable each individual’s health.

Euroherbal is primarily intended to help people feel better and live longer and healthier life.

We are not satisfied with what is expected of us, we always strive to do more than that.

Our goal is to help every person to whom assistance is needed!

Objectives

Through our strategy where we focus on the basic values, trust and responsibility to our customers, employees, partners and the communities in which we live and work on setting up the quality standards. Our purpose is to discover, develop and promote products for prevention and treatment, to contribute to the healthier life, with an in-depth and long-term approach, maintaining at each step the highest ethical standards.

Euroherbal Tea is being committed to integrity, accountability, fairness, transparency and innovation.
